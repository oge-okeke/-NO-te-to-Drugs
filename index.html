Cause and Effect Model!

This animation that we created would be targeted to the the uninformed individuals who aren't that educated on the topic as well as those who know someone who is addicted to drugs.The piece is intended to inform the audience of people the results or effects of drug addiction and how that can ruin someone’s life as well as other different factors.

Structure!

The story starts with an animal (a lion) who is addicted to eating red meat and the cheetah notices it. The story escalates when the lion is overly addicted to red meat (symbolizes pills) and his health starts to decline (noticed by the cheetah) The story ends when the antagonist (the addiction) was ended by the cheetah who started stealing the red meat in order to prevent the lion from continuing the addiction.The change would be helping or stopping addictions that are in the world by assisting the people around you. The change would be lifestyle and changing the way that drugs are perceived in the eyes of the addicted. The events of the world would have been changed by the story in a playful and fun way but it still delivers the message. We want our story to evoke a bad feeling that visually tells the viewers that drug addiction is bad.
